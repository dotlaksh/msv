Features

The school follows CBSE Syllabus. The medium of instruction is English and a good standard of written and spoken English is maintained.
Due importance is given to the National Language. Kannada being the regional language is given equal importance.

The academic session of the school begins from June and ends on March. We have structured and formulated our curriculum on the board based, progressive guidelines laid down by the National Council for Educational Research and Training, New Delhi
